Our Story

Dedicated to Numerological Wisdom

Frazbase was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to make the ancient wisdom of numerology accessible to everyone. Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Frazier, discovered the transformative power of numerology during a spiritual retreat in India.

Today, we're a team of passionate numerologists, spiritual guides, and technology experts working together to bring you accurate numerological insights that can help guide your life decisions and spiritual journey.

We believe that numbers hold the key to understanding the universe's patterns and our place within it. Through our tools, resources, and consultations, we aim to help you discover the hidden meanings in your life's numbers.
